# Issue Template — Hypothesis

```markdown
---
id: "{id}"
title: "{title}"
type: hypothesis
created: {today}
updated: {today}
---

## Objective
One sentence: the question this investigation must answer.

## Hypothesis
The belief being tested: "We believe that {X} is true because {Y}."

## Questions
Questions whose answers would confirm or falsify the hypothesis:
- Q1. {question} → finding that would confirm: {signal}
- Q2. {question} → finding that would confirm: {signal}

## Method
How the investigation will proceed:
- {tool or approach} — {what it will reveal}

## Off-limits
What this investigation will not attempt to answer.

## Done when
- [ ] All questions answered
- [ ] Hypothesis confirmed or falsified with evidence
- [ ] Findings recorded as facts via the `fact` skill

## Facts
Facts this investigation relies on or will produce:
- FACT-NNN — why it matters here

## Context
Background, prior assumptions, related issues or spikes.
```
