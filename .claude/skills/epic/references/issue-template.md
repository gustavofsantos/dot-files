# Issue Template — Epic

```markdown
---
id: "{id}"
title: "{title}"
type: epic
created: {today}
updated: {today}
---

## Objective
One sentence: the large capability or change this epic delivers when all child issues are done.

## Motivation
Why this matters now. The business or technical driver. What breaks or stays broken if we skip it.

## Child Issues
Issues that must be completed to close this epic:
- [ ] #{child-id} — {title}

## Off-limits
What is explicitly out of scope for this epic.

## Done when
- [ ] All child issues closed
- [ ] {integration or system-level condition that only holds when children are complete}

## Facts
Facts this epic relies on (see the `fact` skill):
- FACT-NNN — why it matters here

## Context
Background, constraints, prior decisions, related epics.
```
